Q: How do I register for a program or activity?
A: The Saanich Parks & Recreation App is unable to provide the service of registering for programs and activities at this time. However within a program's information page you may click on 'Register' and follow the instructions given or view our tutorial below.
Q: Some programs don't show a location when I click 'View Location' why is this?
A: Due to the Google Maps API requiring specific location names, it is not uncommon for a program or activity to occasionally not return a result when trying to view a location. Q: Some information regarding programs is not there or set as Unavailable, what does this mean?
A: When populating our application with data we try our hardest to ensure everything you view is correct and up to date, however certain information such as the instructor teaching a program can be very volatile and thus might be unavailable.
