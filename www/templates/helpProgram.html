Introduction
Finding a program or activity is the most essential tool our application can provide, lets quickly run through how it can be done.

Steps
1. Navigate yourself to the Activity browsing page by opening up the main menu and clicking the Activities tab.
2. Once on the activities tab you will be greeted with a listing of all available activity categories, Click on a category you wish to browse.
3. If the category has more than one age group classification you will be greeted with an Age Groups page, if not move on to step 4. Select an age group.
4. Finally you will be provided with a listing of all the programs/activities which corresponded with the Category and Age Group you chose earlier, you may click on any program or activity to view all the information regarding it!
