Introduction
Registration within the Saanich Parks & Rec App is not possible at this time, however it can be used as a means of quickly reaching the registration page located within Rec Online.

Steps to Register
1. Locate a program or activity that you wish to register in by navigating to that programs information page.
2. Click the 'Register' button
3. Copy the barcode displayed on your screen (Write it down or copy it!)
4. Click the 'Go!' button, you will be re-directed to Saanich or another Municipalities RecOnline website.
5. Input your barcode in the input field on RecOnline

And just like that you've begun the registration process! Complete the remaining steps for payment and you will have successfully registered!
